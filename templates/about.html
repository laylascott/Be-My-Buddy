Welcome to Be My Buddy!

If you've ever been apprehensive about walking to your dorm or a bus stop in the dark from the academic section, Be My Buddy is here for you! If you'd like to be a Walker, create an account and request a walk when you need one. A Guide will receive a text with your phone number. If you'd like to be a Guide, create an account and log your availability. When someone requests a walk, you will receive a text with their phone number and the two of you can decide where and when to meet.

Be My Buddy was created at HackViolet, a female-empowerment hackathon at Virginia Tech.
